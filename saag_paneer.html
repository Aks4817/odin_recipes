Authentic Saag Paneer Recipe

    Ingredients
  • 1 (10-ounce) package frozen chopped spinach
  • 1 (10-ounce) package frozen chopped mustard greens
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 (8-ounce) package paneer, cubed

Recipe

Step 1: In a large pot, combine the frozen spinach and mustard greens. Cook over medium heat until they are completely thawed.
Step 2: Remove the greens from the heat and blend them into a smooth paste using a blender or food processor.
Step 3: In a separate pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cumin seeds; saut√© until the onion turns golden brown.
Step 4: Stir in the ginger, turmeric, coriander, cayenne pepper, and salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
Step 5: Add the green paste and water. Cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Step 6: Reduce the heat to low and add milk and heavy cream. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
Step 7: Stir in the garam masala and cardamom.
Step 8: Add the paneer cubes and cook until heated through.
Step 9: Your Authentic Saag Paneer is ready to be served!
