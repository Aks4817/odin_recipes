Aloo Matar Recipe

    Ingredients
  • 2 cups peas (matar)
  • 2 cups potatoes (aloo), diced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Sauté until they crackle.
Step 2: Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
Step 3: Add grated ginger and minced garlic. Sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
Step 4: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate.
Step 5: Add all the dry spices: turmeric, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Sauté for a couple of minutes.
Step 6: Add diced potatoes and peas. Mix well with the masala.
Step 7: Add enough water to cover the vegetables. Season with salt.
Step 8: Cover and simmer until the potatoes and peas are cooked and the curry thickens.
Step 9: Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot.
