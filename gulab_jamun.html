Gulab Jamun Recipe

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • Oil for frying

Recipe

Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, ghee, and baking soda. Mix well.
Step 2: Add milk and knead the mixture into a soft dough. Add cardamom powder and knead again.
Step 3: Divide the dough into small, equal-sized portions and roll them into smooth balls.
Step 4: In a deep saucepan, combine sugar and water. Bring to a boil to make the sugar syrup. Simmer for a few minutes until it reaches a syrupy consistency.
Step 5: Heat oil in a separate pan for frying.
Step 6: Deep fry the dough balls in hot oil until they turn golden brown.
Step 7: Remove the fried balls and immediately immerse them in the sugar syrup.
Step 8: Allow the balls to soak in the syrup for a few hours or until they become soft and sweet.
Step 9: Your Gulab Jamun is ready to be served!
